FAQ

Good to know.

How Userled works, what it plugs into, and how quickly you can be live.

How is Userled different from a traditional ABM platform?

Userled is AI-native: it doesn’t just target accounts, it builds and runs the plays. Agents create personalised microsites, ads and emails for every account and channel, then optimise spend automatically.

Which channels and tools does it work with?

Userled plugs into your CRM, ads, warehouse and workflow tools, with native LinkedIn, email and web personalisation — plus an MCP server and open APIs for everything else.

How does Userled use our first-party data?

It grounds every campaign in your brand kit, knowledge, personas, accounts and contacts — so messaging is on-brand and accurate from the first draft.

How long does it take to get started?

Most teams are live within days. Connect your stack, point Userled at your target accounts, and the agents handle the build.

Is Userled enterprise-ready and secure?

Yes — SOC 2 Type II, SSO & SCIM, audit logs, and EU/US data residency are built in.

Do we need a big team to run it?

No. Teams of one or two run ABM across thousands of accounts — the agents do the heavy lifting while you stay in control.
